Hello, I’m Akang Wang, a third year Ph.D. student in Chemical Engineering (Process Systems Engineering) at Carnegie Mellon University, working with Chrysanthos E. Gounaris. My research is mainly focused on Vehicle Routing Problem, and I’m also interested in theories and applications of Global Optimization.

In this website, you can know more about my research and experience. Feel free to check them out.
